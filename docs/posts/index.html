Arch 40 Case Renders: Switch Plate: 40% Ortholinear + Columnar Stagger Keyboard Inspired by the Ergodox, Atreus, Iris, Corne, and probably others Design goals: Efficiency and minimalism of a 40% form factor Ergo benefits of tilted columnar stagger layout Ease of use coming from ANSI style layouts Simplicity of a non-split keyboard Compatability with Ergodox style keysets Compatible with Cherry MX, Alps, and Kailh Choc switches Assembly: Stacked acrylic layer case, acrylic/metal switch plate Layer dimensions and path lengths are outlined in path_length.
